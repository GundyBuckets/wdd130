Navigating the Tapestry of Time

Delve into the intricacies of the Legend of Zelda timeline, a chronicle that weaves together the fates of heroes across divergent eras. From the Decline Timeline, where Hyrule faces the aftermath of a hero's fall, to the Child Timeline, shaped by a courageous stand against darkness, and the enigmatic waters of the Adult Timeline, each path offers a unique glimpse into the evolving destiny of a kingdom.
